我想冬天和你在温暖的屋子里看雪花飘落
想和你春天阳光明媚的下午在公园漫步
夏天在沙滩边享受海水的清凉
秋天共赏金色落叶的公路

I wish we could watch the winter snow fall in front of a fireplace
Wondering around a park in a bright spring afternoon
Getting away from the hot summer and lying a beach
Driving up the highways with golden maples in the Fall

Every season I want to spend it with you
Every moment I am thinking of you

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Shanghai Typhoon Institute (in collaboration
with Fudan University). My current research explores the alignment process of
weak tropical cyclone under moderate vertical wind shear with dropsonde and
radar observations. My research interest also expands into balance dynamics and
data assimilation.
